# Reasoning & Chain-of-Thought Prompt Templates

## T1 – Zero-shot CoT (universal add-on)
# Append to any question/problem prompt:
Think step by step before giving your final answer.

---

## T2 – Structured CoT (explicit steps)
Solve the following problem. Show your work in this format:
Step 1: [Identify what is known]
Step 2: [Identify what is needed]
Step 3: [Work through the solution]
Step 4: [Verify your answer makes sense]
Final answer: [concise answer]

Problem: {PROBLEM}

---

## T3 – Pros/Cons decision framework
Evaluate the decision: {DECISION}
Context: {CONTEXT}

Structure your analysis as:
Pros:
- <pro 1>
- <pro 2>
Cons:
- <con 1>
- <con 2>
Key risks: <1-2 sentences>
Recommendation: <1 sentence with justification>

---

## T4 – Devil's advocate
I believe: {CLAIM}
Play devil's advocate. Give the 3 strongest counterarguments to my position, then rate how convincing each is (1–5) and why.

---

## T5 – Root cause analysis
A problem occurred: {PROBLEM_DESCRIPTION}
Perform a root cause analysis:
1. Immediate cause: <what directly caused the problem>
2. Contributing factors: <what made it possible>
3. Root cause: <the underlying systemic issue>
4. Prevention: <what would stop it happening again>

---

## T6 – Few-shot CoT template
Solve word problems step by step, then state the final answer clearly.
