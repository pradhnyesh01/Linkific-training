# Summarization Prompt Templates
# Usage: replace {TEXT}, {AUDIENCE}, {LENGTH}, {FORMAT} with your values

## T1 – General constrained summary
Summarize the following for {AUDIENCE} in {LENGTH}:

{TEXT}

---

## T2 – Bullet key facts
Extract the {N} most important facts from the passage below as a concise bullet list. Omit background information.

{TEXT}

---

## T3 – TLDR + Headline
Read the passage and respond with:
Headline: <newspaper-style headline, max 12 words>
TL;DR: <one sentence, max 25 words>

{TEXT}

---

## T4 – Executive summary (structured)
Write an executive summary of the following in this format:
Context: <1 sentence — what this is about>
Key findings: <3 bullet points>
Action items: <bullet points, or "None" if informational>

{TEXT}

---

## T5 – Comparison summary (two documents)
Compare Document A and Document B. Respond with:
- Similarities: <bullet list>
- Key differences: <bullet list>
- Recommendation: <1 sentence conclusion>

Document A:
{TEXT_A}

Document B:
{TEXT_B}
